# Privacy Policy

## What data does the bot collect

- Image content

## Why does it collect said data?

Image content will be used for the browser source of the creator.

## Data visibility

Image content can be found with the creator identifying name.
Image details will be visible to the creator, and their assistant(s).
Images disappear from the carousel and gallery,
when they are gone from discord's databases.

## How long data is stored

Data is stored for a maximum of 3 months.
However there is a 50 fan art limit per creator.
Exceeding 50 will remove the oldest index from the creator's fan art list.
Deletion of data only occurs at a set time every day (time is undetermined for now),
in order to have less issues with malicious people.

## How do you delete your or your fans' data

You can delete your or your fans' data by

1. Logging in as a creator or a creator's assistant, you have access to the images.
   They are listed visually.
2. As a user, ask the person assigned to deal with the fan art.
   The creator is fine to reach out to, if no one is assigned to take care of it.
   Report the creator to discord,
   if there is no way to reach out to remove fan art.

## When your data is collected

You will be asked for permission to use your art in the bot.
Otherwise you may add a nice message of "For the fan art carousel" or similar.

## DMCA and reporting images

Take downs and reports should be handled within the server, and by its moderators.
Creators alone are held liable for this stuff.
Please specify the image by
(a.) linking to the message with the image.
(b.) providing the image link that is used.

## Reporting Creators

Report them to Discord. These guidelines follow discord terms of service
and images are deleted when they are gone from discord.
